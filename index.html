Crystal Collector

How To Play

There is a number goal chosen at random at the start of the game. There are four crystals below. Each have a specific amount of points to add to your score.

You win by matching your total score to the number goal. If you go over the number, you lose.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. Whenever a new game starts, the values of each crystal will change.

Good luck and have fun!
